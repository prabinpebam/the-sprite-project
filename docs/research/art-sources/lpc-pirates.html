LPC Pirates

A large themed clothing-and-props pack - a case study in how the community extends LPC and folds new art straight into the generators.

TL;DR

By bluecarrot16 (commissioned in part by castelonia), LPC Pirates is a full set of pirate outfits and accessories - shirts, vests, coats, hats, sashes, plus injury props like hook hands, peg legs, and eyepatches. Built on ElizaWy's revised bodies and recolored with lpctools, its assets were merged directly into the Universal LPC generator. Licensed CC-BY-SA 3.0 overall, with more permissive options (OGA-BY 3.0) on the author's original pieces.

What's in the pack

Garments

Striped sleeveless and laced shirts, vests/jerkins/waistcoats, collared and frock coats with trim, buttons, epaulets, cuffs, and pockets; pants and baggy pantaloons.

Headwear & accessories

Bicornes, tricornes, bandanas, kerchiefs; cravats and jabots; belts, sashes, and bauldrons - in a wide range of colors.

Injury props

Hook hands, peg legs, and eyepatches for battle-worn plunderers.

Build kit (v1.1)

Source images, instructions, and mask/offset tools for rebuilding repetitive parts and generating recolors with lpctools.

How it reached players

  1. Drawn on revised bodies

    Based on ElizaWy's work and assembled/recolored with lpctools.

  2. Merged upstream

    Pulled into the Universal LPC Spritesheet Generator (castelonia merged the PRs).

  3. Instantly composable

    Now mixes with the rest of the generator's wardrobe - and the tool collects the licenses for you.

Relevance to this project

Pirates is the template for a healthy content pipeline: a commissioned themed pack, built with shared tooling, upstreamed into the generator, and shipped with machine-readable credits. It shows how a sprite platform grows through community + commission, not a single studio.
